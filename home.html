Our Mission

EnerFlow is a student-led initiative dedicated to promoting clean, affordable energy access through innovative technology and widespread awareness campaigns. We are passionately committed to aligning our efforts with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 7, striving for a future where sustainable energy is accessible to all.
