Be a Volunteer

Community Solar Installations

Volunteer to help install solar panels on public buildings like schools or community centers. It promotes clean energy use and lowers electricity costs for the community.

Energy-Efficiency Outreach

Educate households about saving energy through simple changes like using LED bulbs or sealing windows. This helps reduce energy waste and carbon emissions.

Advocacy & Policy Support

Support clean energy laws by joining campaigns, writing letters or spreading awareness. This helps influence decision-makers to support renewable energy initiatives.

Content Creators

Make videos, articles, or social media posts to inform and inspire people about clean energy sources like solar, wind, and hydro to raise awareness and encourage action.
